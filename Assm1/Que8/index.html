Welcome to Ghumo Rajasthan - State Tourism Sites

Rajasthan, the largest state of India, is renowned for its vibrant culture, rich heritage, and majestic forts and palaces. Situated in the northwestern part of the country, Rajasthan is a land of contrasts, where the golden sands of the Thar Desert meet the lush greenery of the Aravalli Range.
