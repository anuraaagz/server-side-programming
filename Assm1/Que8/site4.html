Kumbhalgarh Fort

Kumbhalgarh Fort, nestled in the rugged Aravalli Hills of Rajasthan, stands as a majestic sentinel of history and grandeur. With its expansive walls stretching over 36 kilometers, it is renowned as the second-longest continuous wall in the world, after the Great Wall of China. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, it echoes tales of Rajput valor and architectural brilliance.
